--- title: "38. Berlin Marathon and my first" kind: article created_at: 2011-09-25 21:23:00 author: hardy --- Some random thoughts (of which I had a lot on the 42195 m long track)

  • it holds true:" Wer schneller rennt, hat schneller Pause"
  • it is REALLY a long run
